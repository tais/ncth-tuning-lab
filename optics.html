Optics — scopes, minimum ranges & lasers

Scopes shrink the cone — but only beyond a minimum range. Up close a scope's effective magnification is clamped back toward 1× and you take a "too‑close" aim penalty, so a big scope actually hits worse than iron sights at short range. Each optic has a sweet spot; this shows where. Pick a weapon and compare optics for your shooter.

Faithful to GetScopeMagnificationFactor / CalcMagFactor (range clamp min(mag,(range/NORMAL)/SCOPE_RANGE_MULTIPLIER)), CalcEffectiveMagFactor (skill gate) and the too‑close penalty (Weapons.cpp:6552). Scope mags & weapon stats from Items.xml / Weapons.xml.
