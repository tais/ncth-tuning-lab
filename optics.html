Optics — scopes & lasers

Scopes and lasers shrink the cone of fire. A scope divides the aperture by its magnification — but only as much as the shooter's skill can use (SCOPE_EFFECTIVENESS_*). Lasers shrink the base aperture by a flat % (better in the dark). This is why a low‑skill merc gets far less out of a big scope than a veteran.

Faithful to CalcEffectiveMagFactor / CalcMagFactor and the laser block in AdjustTargetCenterPoint (LOS.cpp).
