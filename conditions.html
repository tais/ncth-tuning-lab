Conditions — hurt, tired, suppressed, drunk

Wounds, fatigue, low morale, suppression shock, drink and gas all reduce CTH — some far more than others. They apply as percentage penalties to both the base chance and the aim span. Suppression shock is the biggest (and low‑exp mercs accrue it fastest), which is why a recruit under fire feels helpless.

Faithful to CalcNewChanceToHitBaseEffectBonus / CalcNewChanceToHitAimEffectBonus (Weapons.cpp).
