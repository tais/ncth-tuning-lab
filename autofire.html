Recoil & Autofire — the muzzle walk

The first bullet of a burst is the aimed shot; every bullet after it adds recoil that pushes the muzzle off target. Strength & agility set how much force you can apply (CalcCounterForceMax); dexterity/wisdom/agility/experience set how accurately you apply it (CalcCounterForceAccuracy). Low‑skill mercs can't tame the climb, so their spray walks off the target.

Simplified conceptual model. It uses the exact CalcCounterForceAccuracy & CalcCounterForceMax formulas but approximates the per‑bullet correction loop of CalcRecoilOffset (real muzzle-walk is 2‑axis and gun-specific). Directions & levers are faithful.
