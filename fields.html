Gun XML fields — what NCTH actually reads per weapon

CTHConstants.ini tunes the system; each gun brings its own stats from Weapons.xml / Items.xml. Two of those fields have OCTH and NCTH twins — tune the wrong one and nothing happens. Below: the trap table, then a live chart per field showing what changing it does (shooter: exp / marks , standing, iron sights, current tuning).

⚠️ The twin-field traps

What you want to changeOCTH reads…NCTH reads…Notes
Gun accuracy / spreadbAccuracy (0–~15)nAccuracy (0–100) Under NCTH, bAccuracy is completely ignored (Items.cpp:14338). nAccuracy is also scaled by gun condition and boosted by ammo/attachment PercentAccuracyModifier.
Burst / autofire spreadubBurstPenaltybRecoilX + bRecoilY NCTH replaced the flat burst penalty with a per-bullet recoil vector (X = sideways pull, Y = climb) plus ubRecoilDelay. First bullet is always recoil-free.
Handling / bulkHandlingBiggest per-gun NCTH factor: base/aim CTH penalty ×stance ×DRAW_COST.
Aiming depthubAimLevelsMax aim clicks; NCTH only reaches the CTH cap by spending them all.
Effective rangeusRangeBullet-deviation range steps, AI shot limit, gravity onset. Displayed in meters (1 tile = 10 m).
Attachments & ammo add: PercentCap (aimed ceiling — 311 items!), PercentHandling, PercentMaxCounterForce/PercentCounterForceAccuracy (recoil control), PercentAccuracyModifier, Range(Percent)Bonus, ScopeMagFactor/BestLaserRange. Per weapon-type global multipliers live in Item_Settings.ini.
